# SmartShield - AI-Powered Phishing Protection

## Short Description (52 characters)
AI-powered phishing protection for your browser

## Full Description

### Protect Yourself from Phishing Attacks

SmartShield is an intelligent browser extension that shields you from phishing, malware, and fraudulent websites in real-time. Using advanced AI-powered risk assessment, SmartShield analyzes websites as you browse to detect suspicious behavior and alert you before you become a victim.

### Key Features

**Real-Time Website Scanning**
- Instant analysis of every website you visit
- Checks domain reputation, registration details, and security indicators
- Smart caching prevents redundant checks for faster performance

**AI-Powered Risk Detection**
- Advanced machine learning models identify phishing patterns
- Detection of spoofed domains, newly registered suspicious sites, and known malware hosts
- Explainable AI shows you exactly why a site was flagged

**Browser Integration**
- One-click access from your browser toolbar
- Non-intrusive notifications only when threats are detected
- Works silently in the background with minimal performance impact

**Privacy-First Design**
- No tracking of your browsing history
- All scans are processed securely
- Your data stays under your control

### How It Works

1. **Browsing** - Visit websites normally
2. **Analysis** - SmartShield analyzes each domain in real-time
3. **Protection** - Get instant alerts for suspicious or malicious sites
4. **Safety** - Browse with confidence

### Safe Mode

Toggle Safe Mode on/off from the extension popup to control when SmartShield actively scans websites. Perfect for trusted networks or when you need to temporarily disable checks.

### About the Technology

SmartShield leverages:
- Real-time whois and DNS lookups
- Machine learning-based risk assessment
- Domain reputation analysis
- Known phishing and malware databases

### Need Help?

SmartShield is continuously updated with the latest threat intelligence to protect you against emerging phishing tactics and attack vectors.

---

**Permissions Used:**
- `activeTab` - to analyze the current webpage
- `tabs` - to monitor browser navigation
- `storage` - to save your Safe Mode preferences
- `notifications` - to alert you of threats
- `alarms` - to manage scan operations

**No unused permissions.** SmartShield requests only the access it needs to protect you.
